Group 6 Project: Biorxiv Literature Analytics

Welcome to the Watspeed Group 6 Literature Analytics Project! This project aims to provide tools for analyzing scientific literature, specifically focusing on abstracts and summaries.

We have developed two main tools:

  • Abstract Query Tool: Paste an abstract to find similar articles and gain insights.
  • Literature Summary Tool: Generate a summary of recent literature based on weekly updates.
